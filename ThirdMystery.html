The Story Begins

It was the last working Saturday at Vidya Mandir School. Arjun, a curious Class 10 student, discovered a dusty cupboard in the old chemistry lab. Inside was a torn lab manual titled:

"Only those who decode this, shall pass the test of the elements and currents"

THE ELEMENT'S SECRET

"When quick lime meets water, heat and white powder form. When limestone faces fire, it breaks apart releasing the breath of life."

DECODE THIS: Write the combination reaction when CaO reacts with water, and the decomposition reaction when CaCO is heated.

[The product of the combination reaction is your first code]

Challenge 2 of 4

THE CURRENT'S PATH

"Two glowing orbs, one resists 10, other 20. When 6 volts push through their series dance, what current flows?"

DECODE THIS: Calculate the current when 10 and 20 resistors are connected in series with a 6V battery.

[Your answer in amperes is the second part of the code]

Challenge 3 of 4

THE NUMBER'S RIDDLE

"A rectangular garden's length exceeds its width by 2 meters. If the garden covers exactly 15 square meters, what is its width?"

DECODE THIS: Form a quadratic equation and solve to find the width of the garden in meters.

[The width value is the third part of the code]

Challenge 4 of 4

THE WORD'S WISDOM

"Count the guardians that stand before nouns in this message: '_ student found _ laboratory with _ amazing secret and _ hidden treasure.'"

DECODE THIS: Fill the blanks with correct articles (a, an, the) and count how many times you used "the".

[The count of "the" is the final part of the code]

THE ULTIMATE CODE

Combine your four answers in order:

[Chemical Formula] - [Current Value] - [Garden Width] - [Article Count]

Example format: MgO-0.2-3-2
